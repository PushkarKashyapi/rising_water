Emergency Tips & Safety Guidelines

In case of a flood, always stay informed by following official weather alerts and updates. If water levels start rising, act fast and move to higher ground without waiting for instructions. Keep an emergency kit ready with essentials like clean water, non-perishable food, a flashlight, and important documents to stay safe and prepared.

Past Flood data

Past flood data reveals how quickly water levels can rise and how devastating the impact can be on lives and infrastructure. Analyzing historical flood patterns helps identify high-risk zones, peak seasons, and vulnerable communities. This data is crucial for building early warning systems, planning evacuation routes, and improving future emergency response strategies.

Live Weather

Live weather data plays a critical role in flood prediction by providing real-time updates on rainfall intensity, river levels, and storm patterns. It enables timely alerts and quick decision-making to minimize damage and ensure public safety. Continuous monitoring of live conditions helps authorities and communities respond faster and more effectively during emergencies.
