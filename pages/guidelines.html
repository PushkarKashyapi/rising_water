BEFORE A FLOOD: Be Prepared

Stay Informed

Sign up for local weather alerts or flood warning systems.
Follow news from the Meteorological Department or NDMA.

Create an Emergency Kit

Water, dry food, flashlight, batteries, medications, first aid, cash, ID copies, power bank.

Plan an Evacuation Route

Know the safest path to higher ground.
Practice drills with your family.

Protect Important Documents

Keep them in waterproof pouches or store digitally.

Flood-proof Your Home

Elevate electrical appliances, seal basement walls, install backflow valves in toilets.
